# AI Girlfriend Streamlit App

This is a Streamlit application that lets you chat with your AI Girlfriend powered by PygMalion.

## How to deploy:

1. Push this repository to GitHub.
2. Log in to Streamlit Cloud (https://streamlit.io/).
3. Connect Streamlit with your repository.
4. Streamlit will deploy the application.
5. You'll get a link to chat directly with your AI Girlfriend.

✨ Enjoy! ❤️
